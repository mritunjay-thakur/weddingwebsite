Our Story

Traditional Roots, Modern Vision

Founded in 2002, Thakur Events & Weddings began with a simple mission: to preserve the beauty of Indian wedding traditions while adapting them for contemporary couples.

Our team of seasoned designers, cultural experts, and event specialists work together to create celebrations that honor your heritage while reflecting your personal style.
